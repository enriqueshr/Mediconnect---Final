Plain English Summary: We are a healthcare platform. We collect only what we need to connect you with doctors. Your medical information is encrypted, never sold, and protected under HIPAA. If you are in the EU/UK, GDPR applies and you have full data rights including the right to export or erase your data.

1. Who We Are

MediConnect ("we," "us," "our") is a digital health platform connecting patients with licensed healthcare providers. We act as:

  • Data Controller (GDPR Art. 4(7)) for all user account and appointment data.
  • Covered Entity / Business Associate (HIPAA 45 CFR §160.103) for Protected Health Information.

Our registered address and Data Protection Officer contact appear in Section 12.

2. What Data We Collect

2.2 Health Data (PHI)

  • Appointment details (date, time, doctor, visit type)
  • Reason for visit and clinical notes (AES-256 encrypted at rest)

  • IP address, browser user-agent (for security and audit logs)
  • Authentication tokens (JWT, stored client-side; refresh tokens hashed server-side)
  • Login attempt history (for account lockout protection)
  • Consent records with timestamp and IP

4. Protected Health Information (HIPAA)

MediConnect is designed to comply with the HIPAA Privacy Rule (45 CFR Part 164) and Security Rule. Specifically:

  • Encryption at Rest: PHI fields (appointment reason, clinical notes, messages) are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before storage.
  • Encryption in Transit: All communications use TLS 1.2 or higher in production.
  • Minimum Necessary: Staff and systems access only the PHI needed for their role.
  • Audit Controls (§164.312(b)): All PHI access is logged immutably with user, timestamp, IP, and action.
  • Automatic Logoff (§164.312(a)(2)(iii)): Sessions expire after 15 minutes of inactivity.
  • Unique User Identification (§164.312(a)(2)(i)): Every user has a unique ID; role-based access control is enforced.
Breach Notification: In the event of a breach affecting your PHI, we will notify you and HHS within 60 days (HIPAA §164.408) or 72 hours for EU residents (GDPR Art. 33).

5. How We Share Data

We never sell your personal data. We share data only as follows:

  • Doctors you book with: They see your name, contact, and appointment reason to provide care.
  • Payment processors (eSewa/Khalti): We share only the minimum data required for payment processing. No card numbers are stored by MediConnect.
  • Legal requirements: We disclose data when required by law, court order, or to prevent imminent harm.
  • Business Associates: Cloud hosting, backup, and monitoring vendors who sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs).

6. Security & Encryption

  • PHI Field Encryption: AES-256-GCM with unique IV per record
  • Password Hashing: bcrypt with cost factor 12
  • Token Security: JWT (HS256) + refresh tokens stored as SHA-256 hashes
  • Account Lockout: 5 failed login attempts triggers a 30-minute lockout
  • Security Headers: HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options, Referrer-Policy via Helmet.js
  • Rate Limiting: Auth endpoints limited to 10 req/15 min; API to 200 req/15 min
  • Audit Logging: Immutable access log with 6-year retention

8. Your Rights

GDPR Rights (EU/UK Users)

  • Right of Access (Art. 15): Export all your data at Profile → Download My Data or contact us.
  • Right to Erasure (Art. 17): Request anonymisation of your personal data. Medical records are de-identified (not deleted) per healthcare law.
  • Right to Portability (Art. 20): Your data export is in machine-readable JSON format.
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16): Update your profile in-app or contact us.
  • Right to Object (Art. 21): Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Restrict (Art. 18): Request restriction of processing while a dispute is resolved.

CCPA Rights (California Residents)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected and how it's used
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

HIPAA Rights (US Patients)

  • Right to access your PHI (45 CFR §164.524)
  • Right to request amendment of PHI (§164.526)
  • Right to an accounting of disclosures (§164.528)
  • Right to request restrictions on PHI use (§164.522)
To exercise any of these rights, use the in-app controls (Profile page) or email dpo@mediconnect.com. We will respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 30–60 days (HIPAA).

9. Cookies & Tracking

We use only essential cookies/local storage:

  • Authentication token: JWT stored in localStorage — required for login. Expires in 24 hours.
  • Refresh token: Stored in localStorage, hashed on server. Expires in 7 days.
  • Consent record: mc_consent_v1 — your cookie preference.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party advertising trackers.

10. International Data Transfers

MediConnect is hosted in [your region]. If you access this service from the EU/EEA, your data may be transferred to and processed in a country that may not have the same data protection laws. We implement appropriate safeguards:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU→third-country transfers
  • Adequacy decisions where available
  • End-to-end encryption for PHI (reduces transfer risk)

11. Children's Privacy

MediConnect is not directed to children under 13 (US/COPPA) or under 16 (EU/GDPR). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Pediatric services require a parent or guardian to create the account. Contact us immediately if you believe we have collected data from a minor.
